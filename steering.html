Steering Committee

GGF was supported by a steering committee made up of international senior policymakers, academics and private sector representatives from the nine participating countries – Brazil, China, France, Germany, India, Indonesia, Japan, South Africa, and the United States.

Members of the steering committee made the final selection of GGF fellows. The committee assisted GGF by providing feedback on the overall program as well as concrete input on the policy areas covered. During the working group sessions, members of the steering committee supported the fellows by joining the discussions and sharing their expertise.
